Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about our trips, bookings, payments, and more. We’re here to make your GoSafari Nepal experience smooth and hassle-free.

What is GoSafari?

GoSafari is a 3-wheeler ride aggregator in Biratnagar that connects passengers with nearby tempo drivers through a user-friendly app, offering reliable, safe, and affordable transportation.

How do I book a ride with GoSafari?

Simply download the GoSafari app, enable location, and tap to book a ride. The app finds the nearest available 3-wheeler and connects you instantly with the driver.

How can I join as a 3-wheeler driver?

Drivers can register through our app or visit our office in Biratnagar. After verification, you'll be able to accept ride requests and earn directly through the platform.

Is GoSafari safe to use?

Absolutely. All our drivers go through a verification process, and our system tracks rides in real time. We prioritize both passenger and driver safety at every step.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about booking rides, payments, features, and using GoSafari in Biratnagar.

Q. How do I book a ride?

Just open the GoSafari app, enter your pickup and drop-off locations, and tap on “Book Now.” A nearby 3-wheeler driver will be assigned to you instantly.

Q. How can I pay for the ride?

You can pay in cash directly to the driver after the ride. We're also working on integrating digital payment options for added convenience.

Q. Are the fares fixed or negotiable?

All fares on GoSafari are pre-calculated based on distance and time — so you get fair and transparent pricing with no need to negotiate.

Q. Is GoSafari available all over Biratnagar?

Yes, GoSafari covers all major areas of Biratnagar. You can check coverage in the app while booking a ride.

Q. How does GoSafari help drivers?

GoSafari connects drivers with more ride requests, helping them earn better and avoid long waiting times. We also assist with route navigation and customer support.

Q. Is my location and data safe?

Yes, your data is protected. We use secure systems to safeguard your location and ride history. We never share your personal information without your permission.
