How GoSafariNepal Works

GoSafari Nepal makes travel easier and smarter. Our platform connects you directly with reliable local 3-wheeler drivers, helping you find, book, and enjoy safe and affordable rides.

Search

Start your journey by exploring available three-wheeler rides or services. Use our smart filters to choose your destination, preferred driver, or type of vehicle – all based on your needs.

Book

Once you find a suitable ride or driver, book instantly through our platform. No middlemen, no confusion – just direct, transparent booking.

Instruct

Give clear instructions about pickup time, location, or any special requests. Our built-in messaging system makes it easy to communicate directly with your driver.

Pay

Once the ride is complete, pay securely through the app or in person. Your safety and satisfaction are our priorities.

Empowering Biratnagar’s local transport with a smart, easy-to-use platform that connects passengers and 3-wheeler drivers for safe, reliable, and affordable rides.
