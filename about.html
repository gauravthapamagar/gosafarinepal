About Us

GoSafari is Biratnagar’s smart 3-wheeler ride aggregator—designed to simplify urban travel. We connect passengers and drivers through a seamless app experience, offering fast bookings, fair pricing, and reliable rides. Our mission is to digitally empower local transport and make everyday commuting safer, easier, and more efficient for all.
